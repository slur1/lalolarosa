About Us

Behind the brand

Why we do what we do

Hola! Shaira Rose here — the heart behind La Lola Rosa.

This all started as a dream. Ever since I was young, I knew I wanted to build something of my own. My biggest inspiration? my mom. I grew up watching her pour love into our small family business, it was our bread and butter and a big part of who I am today.

Why we do what we do

From selling ballers in 6th grade to bags and clothes, entrepreneurship has always been part of me. Even when I entered the corporate world, I stayed close to sales, because connecting with people and creating something meaningful has always felt like home.

Why we do what we do

Then in 2023, I got married and everything clicked. I fell in love with the magic of events: the planning, the emotions, and all the beautiful details that make each moment unforgettable. It made me realize how deeply people pour themselves into their once-in-a-lifetime celebrations and I knew I wanted to be part of that.

That’s how La Lola Rosa was born.

We’re here to help you hold onto the magic of your event with keepsakes crafted to last a lifetime.

Because after all the wedding preparations, you deserve something beautiful to take home.
